Tangier is located in the northernmost part of Morocco, at the western entrance to the Strait of Gibraltar. It is approximately 350 kilometers (about 220 miles) north of the country's capital, Rabat. Tangier is easily accessible by road, with major highways connecting it to cities like Rabat, Casablanca, and Fes.
