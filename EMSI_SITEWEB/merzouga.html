Merzouga is approximately 550 kilometers (about 340 miles) southeast of the city of Marrakech. The journey by road typically takes around 9-10 hours by car, passing through beautiful landscapes and small towns along the way. Alternatively, travelers can opt for domestic flights from Marrakech to nearby towns such as Errachidia or Ouarzazate, followed by a shorter drive to Merzouga.
