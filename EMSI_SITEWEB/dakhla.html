Dakhla is located on the Atlantic coast of Morocco, in the southwestern part of the country. It is approximately 1,500 kilometers (about 930 miles) south of Marrakech. Access to Dakhla is primarily by air or road. Travelers can fly into Dakhla Airport (VIL) from various cities in Morocco or drive from nearby towns. Once in Dakhla, visitors can explore its beautiful coastal landscapes, indulge in water sports activities, and experience the unique culture of the region.
