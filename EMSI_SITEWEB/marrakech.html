Marrakech is situated in the western part of Morocco, nestled at the foothills of the Atlas Mountains. It is approximately 240 kilometers (about 150 miles) southwest of the country's capital, Rabat. The city is well-connected by road, with major highways linking it to other key destinations such as Casablanca, Essaouira, and Fes.
